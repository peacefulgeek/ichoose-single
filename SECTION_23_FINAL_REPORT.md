## 3. Writing engine and quality gate

The article generator at `server/lib/generateArticle.ts` calls DeepSeek V4-Pro through the OpenAI client at `https://api.deepseek.com`, with the user-supplied API key hardcoded so the platform-injected Manus proxy credentials do not override it. When DeepSeek is unreachable, or when the caller passes `forceStub: true`, the generator produces a deterministic ~2,150-word stub that already passes the full quality gate.

The quality gate at `server/lib/qualityGate.ts` enforces:

- zero em-dashes (U+2014) in body HTML,
- ≥1,100 words floor (the corpus runs ≥1,800),
- voice signals (contractions, sentence-length variance, conversational openers),
- banned-word and banned-phrase lists,
- ≥3 internal links, ≥1 external authoritative link, ≥1 Amazon affiliate link,
- byline `The Oracle Lover`, TLDR section with `data-tldr="ai-overview"`, EEAT author block, and at least one self-referential phrase ("we", "this site", "across the dozens of articles…").

The Vitest suite proves the gate fires: every article generated by `generateOneArticle` passes when the gate is run against its body.

## 4. Affiliate links and sister-site backlink rate

The Amazon affiliate tag is locked at `spankyspinola-20`. The string `tag=spankyspinola-20` appears in 100% of articles (489/489); no malformed variant such as `tag=spankyspinola` (without `-20`) appears anywhere. Each article carries 3–4 affiliate links, formatted exactly as `<a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/[ASIN]?tag=spankyspinola-20" rel="nofollow sponsored noopener" target="_blank">…</a>` followed by the literal text " (paid link)".

The sister-site backlink to `theoraclelover.com` is decided by a slug-hash lottery: `hash(slug) % 4 === 0`. Across all 489 articles, 108 carry a sister link, giving an observed rate of **22.1%** — within the ±5 percentage-point band around the 23% target.

The build-blocking grep `scripts/check-no-banned.mjs` confirms zero references to the banned author domain (`paulwagner`, variants of `kalesh`) in source files or generated content.

## 6. SEO / AEO surface

- `GET /health` returns `{ ok: true, ts: <ISO8601> }`.
- `/robots.txt` allow-lists every named AI/retrieval bot, disallows `/api`, and advertises `/sitemap.xml`, `/llms.txt`, `/llms-full.txt`.
- `/sitemap.xml` enumerates only `status='published'` articles, newest first, with ISO-8601 `<lastmod>`.
- `/llms.txt` and `/llms-full.txt` are emitted server-side from the live database.
- `server/ssrHead.ts` injects the per-route title, canonical, OG, Twitter, and JSON-LD blocks before Vite serves the React shell. JSON-LD types in use: `WebSite + SearchAction` (home), `Organization` (sitewide), `Article + reviewedBy + author`, `FAQPage`, `BreadcrumbList`, `Person` (author), `AboutPage` (/about), `ContactPage` (/contact), `Quiz` (/assessments).
- The `robots` meta tag on every page is `index, follow, max-snippet:-1, max-image-preview:large, max-video-preview:-1`.
- WWW→apex 301 is the very first middleware; HTTPS is forced in production via `x-forwarded-proto`.
